Deliverable: Technical Specification Report

Scenario: You are in a church sound booth. You have 3 minutes to summarize the sermon notes below. Focus is critical.

The rapid integration of neural rhythms requires a precise alignment of the thalamocortical loop. If the external frequency matches the endogenous alpha rhythm, the subject's ability to maintain top-down executive control is severely compromised. This leads to a state of 'haze' or 'brain fog' where the subject can see the text but cannot parse its semantic value.
