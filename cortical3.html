PHYSICAL DEGRADATION PROTOCOL

The sensation of nausea in response to stroboscopic stimuli is primarily a result of visuo-vestibular conflict. When the luminance of a display oscillates between metameric whites, the autonomic nervous system is triggered as if the body has ingested a toxin. This creates an involuntary 'haze' where the brain's internal model of reality no longer matches sensory input. In an interrogation environment, this 'biological noise' serves to erode the subject's willpower, as the metabolic cost of maintaining a coherent narrative exceeds the brain's current energy capacity.
